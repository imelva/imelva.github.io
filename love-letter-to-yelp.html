Basic photo editing for smoother posting experiences and better content
Many restaurants have really dark lighting for atmosphere which directly results in low photo quality. Unlike other social apps, Yelp doesnt seem to have an editing feature. Yes, maybe it helps reflect the true image of food. Yet, it mostly doesn’t. Better images help other users to know more and be more open about exploring. Customized suggestion and targeting
From my experiences, Yelp’s suggestion is mainly based on locations. Yet there are many more components when it comes to choices. Eventually, it would be really cool to make suggestions based on relationship status, preferences, ethnities etc. This can also be applied to Yelp’s business solution. More targeted ads always result in better conversions and hence revenue.

Themed suggestions
Meanwhile, themed content based on the time would also raise the engagement a lot. Creating a user habit where they not only come to Yelp when they need to to but would casually browse like browsing news. For example, today is the Lunar Festival + blood moon. Imagine if we can browse which restaurants have a patio that can be good to watch the moon.
