One of my projects - Portfolio Health Automation


Problem

Given GSO staff’s busy schedules and other priorities, enabling them to improve clients’ ad portfolios effortlessly


Context

As a company, the sales strategy is to help our clients gain more value through their ads so we can gain more profits on the long run. However, the sales teams often neglect this and focus more on their immediate sales quota.


Metrics

Facebook has its own metrics to evaluate how efficient and effective our advertisers are bidding on our platforms:

  • Direct Response Optimality: Indicator to show if a direct response ad (targeting on things like website visits, conversions etc.) is bidding optimally.

  • Brand Optimality: Indicator to show if a brand ad (targeting on things like brand awareness) is bidding optimally.

These two indicators take into account many factors such as the alignment between ad product and ad objective and clients’ pixel/conversion tracking. Based on the characteristics of different industries in different geos, there are standards of what’s considered as optimal. The metrics we will use to evaluate the success in this case would be the % of DR Optimal and % of Brand Optimal.


Research

  • Investigated clients’ portfolio health by industries, companies, impact sizes etc.

  • Analyzed common hurdles that clients encounter and sort solution kit accordingly

  • Surveyed and Interviewed sales representatives from various industries


Solution

Built an automated reporting system that would monitor and summarize clients’ portfolio health and rank issues by dollar impact. Customized emails with accounts, issues, dollar impacts and solutions would be sent out on a scale to account managers that they can then easily prioritize and forward those reports to their clients to facilitate any improvements.


Result

  • Achieved 30% increase on direct response optimality and 9% increase on brand optimality.

  • Moved both stats from underperforming to be over-goal within a month

ONE OF MY PROJECTS - CREDIT COACHING ACTIONS


Context

50%+ of credit karma users come to the site to only to improve their scredit scores. However, the company currently only displays free credit scores along with generic credit factor information. A lot of users especially users with limited understanding about credit find those overwhelming.


Problem

  • Help members to understand what it takes to improve their credit scores and encourage them to act upon those.

  • The side effect of this should create better retention for the company in the long term.


Metrics

  • Feature reach: Number of eligible members

  • Members' take rate on those actions

  • Feature Retention

  • Overall Retention


Solution

Built a credit coaching feature that provides members targeted action items to take to improve their score.

Action cards provide a completed experience from suggestion, status updates to completion feedbacks.
