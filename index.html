Some Highlights

  • 2005

    Spoke at multiple conferences & provincial congress

    Youngest presenter at the first Shandong Annual News Figure Award, Speaker at the Congress of Representative Women,Youth reperesentative in Provincial Youth Conference etc.

  • 2006

    Got accepted to Renmin University of China for Journalism

    Was selected as a youth reporter from over 600,000 applicants

  • 2009

    Volunteered teaching in rural China

    Seeing how individual's life can differ so much by their surroundings and education had striked me. Since then, I started to have a very strong urge to better opportunities for those who are in need

  • 2010

    Started my journey in Canada

    This was a crazy phase of my life. I had manage 3 part-time jobs, extra-curriculums, school, adapting to a new country and babysitting my 2-year-old brother at the same time. What doesn't kill you make you stronger :)

  • 2013

    Got into Entreprenurship & University of Waterloo

    Many great things have happened in Waterloo. I've worked for many cool places as well as my own venture

  • 2014

    Founded Spice Creation and co-founded Soapbox and Kairos Society in Canada

    Again, an exciting year. I founded Spice Creation and have grown it to a team of 6 and 15 freelancers. I've also co-founded Soapbox, which later got integrated to UN Millenium Development Goals

  • 2015

    Co-Founded ConNextor, exhibited at Collision Conference & Got in Launchpad

    Moving from discovery to early sales, we've worked with many exciting companies including Quora, Uber and Square. We've pitched in front of 2000 people at the collision conference, got into both Launchpad & Velocity and got a free officespace in downtown kitchener with Communitech.
